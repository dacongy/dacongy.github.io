Our paper Static Control-Flow Analysis of User-Driven Callbacks in Android Applications has been accepted to appear in ICSE'15.

About Me

Currently, I'm a Software Engineer at Google. I am interested in compiler technologies, software performance, and mobile computing. Feel free to contact me if you need a reviewer/referee for academic conferences or journals in the field of programming languages and software engineering.
